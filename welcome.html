Designed for All Abilities

HealthTrack meets WCAG 2.1 Level AA across every screen. Colour contrast ratios are verified at a minimum of 4.5:1 for body text, all interactive elements carry visible focus indicators, and no information is conveyed through colour alone.

Our interface follows the four POUR principles: every feature is Perceivable, Operable, Understandable, and Robust across assistive technologies.

Perceivable Operable Understandable Robust
  • Full Keyboard Navigation Every feature is reachable and operable using only a keyboard, with visible focus rings on all interactive elements.
  • Screen Reader Compatible Semantic HTML5, ARIA landmarks, live regions, and descriptive labels ensure complete compatibility with NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver.
  • Verified Colour Contrast All text meets or exceeds 4.5:1 contrast ratio. Status is communicated with icons and labels, never colour alone.
  • Text Resize Support Layouts reflow correctly at 200% zoom with no loss of content or functionality, meeting WCAG 1.4.4 and 1.4.10.
  • Reduced Motion Support All animations and transitions are disabled automatically when the user has enabled "Reduce Motion" in their operating system.
